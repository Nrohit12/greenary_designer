About.

This is not your usual internship or project. Its about how fascinated you are in learning and applying the learnt concepts to create a unique application and website design.

Responsibilities:

  • Working closely with the developer to finalise the designs.
  • Completing the complete Mobile and Website App design.
  • Creating a Word or Slides doc on Google to keep a track of the designs.

Good to have Skills.

Mobile and Website Knowledge

A bit Knowledge of different components such as Menu Bar, Drawer, Navigation Bar etc. Need not know their working but a bit idea of where they are located else ready to learn that.

Designing Softwares

Good to have skills on any designing software, whether it be Adobe Photoshop or Canva else ready to learn them. Also, simple designs creation using Paint is also preferred.

Other Technical Knowledge

Have you ever used Sketch Cloud? Well, if you have then, you are the right choice. But if you don't get ready to work on it. Also, good to have Google Docs and Slides knowledge.

Just remember that you are not required to have all the above technical and designing knowledge. But you must be ready to learn them. It is always preferred to have good imagination skills. You must also be willing to do some research on various other designs already being used.
